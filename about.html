► What is TFMC? ◄

What is TFMC you may ask? Its a surrealistic roleplaying game where you venture through dreamlike landscapes, meeting strange and unique characters in your journey!

✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶

★ The Journey ★

In this wicked journey you will face multiple different quests and battles as you explore this weird world, helping others, or completely ignoring them! Whatever is best for your quest, after all, its the reason you are there!

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

★ Your Objective ★

Your objective in this game as the player, a soul inside a vessel, is to reach an entity called The Star, who can fulfill any wish in the world, no matter how unattainable it seems, she can do it!

✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶✶

► Combat ◄

On your journey, you will win battles with enemies, with bullet hell sections inspired by games like Enter the Gungeon, The Binding of Isaac and obviously, Undertale. And they come with a parry mechanic, where you can deflect enemy bullets and turn them into will points, which can be spent on magic spells!

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

★ Your Choices Matter ★

And before you ask, yes! Battles can end in different outcomes other than death, you can (or try to) be reasonable with your foes and hope you can end the battle in a peaceful manner. Of course this means that, depending on your choices, outcomes in the game may change, but be aware, in this world, nothing is as white and black as you may think.
